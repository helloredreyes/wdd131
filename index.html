THE PHILIPPINES

The Philippines is an archipelago located in Southeast Asia, composed of over 7,000 islands. It is known for its stunning beaches, diverse marine life, and vibrant culture. The capital city, Manila, is a bustling metropolis that blends modernity with rich historical heritage. It is known for its warm and hospitable people as well. The coral reefs in many islands are some of the most diverse in the world making it great for scuba diving and snorkling. I recommend snorkling in Boracay, Palawan and Cebu. Filipino and English are the official languages.
